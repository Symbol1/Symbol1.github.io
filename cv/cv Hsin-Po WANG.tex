\sec{Research Interests}

\def\interest#1{\textcolor{red!20!black}{\bfseries#1}}
\def\work#1{\textcolor{yellow!30!black}{\bfseries#1}}
\def\apply#1{\textcolor{green!20!black}{\bfseries#1}}

I am interested in
\interest{information theory} and
\interest{coding theory}.
I have worked on
\work{polar codes} (for wireless communication), 
\work{regenerating codes} (for cloud storage),
\work{distributed matrix--matrix multiplication} (for cloud computation),
\work{group testing}, and
\work{DNA data storage}.
I specialize in finding applications of
\apply{algebra},
\apply{combinatorics},
\apply{calculus},
\apply{probability theory},
and other mathematical tools to said topics.
